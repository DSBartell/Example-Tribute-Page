~ Once Known as Lord Griffith

Griffith was born a poor pauper with nearly nothing. One day he met a strange woman who wore a strange token called a Behelit, Egg of the King around her neck. After much distant admiration, young Griffith inquired about the token. Little did he know, whoever had it was destined to one day make a choice between every single one of their friends, aquaintances and lovers and tuly unlimited power.

Though Griffith wasn't particularly superstitious, the so called prophecy was enough for him to hold on to it as a good luck charm. He fought fearlessly to ascend through the ranks of both mercenaries and the royal army, meeting many a friend and foe along the way. It appeared eventually that the only way Griffith was going was up- leading to many assasination plots. Every single one was trounced by his friend Guts.
